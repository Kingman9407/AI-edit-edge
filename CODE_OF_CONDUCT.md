# Community Guidelines & Code of Conduct

Welcome to our project! We want to keep things simple, open, and collaborative. 

## 🤝 Our Pledge
We encourage everyone to learn, experiment, and contribute. We are building a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment. Be kind, be respectful, and focus on helping each other grow.

## 🚫 Non-Commercial Use Policy
This project is created for the community, for educational purposes, and for open collaboration. **You may not use, repurpose, or distribute this project, its code, or its models for commercial purposes or monetization of any kind.**

If you wish to use this for a commercial product or service, please reach out to the project maintainers directly to discuss permissions.

## ✅ Expected Behavior
* **Be respectful:** Treat others with kindness and empathy.
* **Be collaborative:** Share your knowledge, submit pull requests, and help others.
* **Be constructive:** Provide and gracefully accept constructive feedback.

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior
* Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments of any kind.
* Any attempt to monetize, sell, or commercially exploit the code, models, or services derived from this repository without explicit written permission.
* Spamming or trolling.

## 📬 Reporting & Enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or notice someone violating the non-commercial policy, please report it to the maintainers at [aseemnajeer@gmail] We will take appropriate action to ensure the project remains a safe, collaborative, and strictly non-commercial space.
